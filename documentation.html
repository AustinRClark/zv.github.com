Artifact

Artifact is a highly performant, scalable distributed key-value store system, based on Amazon's Dynamo database model. It's open source and abhorrently available. Unstructured and permissive in what it accepts, strict in what it delivers -- perfect for storing instantaneously accessible session information, massive datasets and deep analytics.
Artifact is currently commercially used by MixRank and Primer Labs to deliver wickedly fast record retrieval without write locks.
