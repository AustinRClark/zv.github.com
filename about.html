Swiftcrack

My first open source project, replete with embarrasing mistakes that remind me how much I've grown since 14! This uses patched WUSB54GS drivers and h1kar1s excellent `chop-chop` package to make one of the very first WEP crackers for Windows. Surprisingly, it lives on despite the presence of considerably more advanced tools like aircrack.
