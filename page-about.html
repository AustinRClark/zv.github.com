Plot Synopsis

Set in the near future, A New Kind of Sentience follows the bizarre exploits of Zephyr Pellerin, who lives an isolated existence in a fortified mountain hideaway - an exile from the human community.

Following a call from his irate manager demanding the last two modules per his publishing deal, Zephyr packs his belongings and descends the mountains before traveling to the classic city of iniquity -- San Francisco; a twisted amalgam of pervasive consumerism, sex, violence, drugs and the destitute homeless. One of God's prototype cities - A high powered liberal mutant Mecca of technology never even remotely considered for mass production, but too wierd let perish in a fiery apocalypse.

Now, he addresses the disenfranchised and alienated citizens of the Bay and beyond, acknowledging their deep disappointment in the 'future' they live in, terrified of a world slipping deeper into irreconcilible catastrophe. Now, everyone is listening and watching closely as he heralds a new digital prophet: the thinking machines -- the so called final technology -- the last shard of hope that everyone is looking to to save us from ourselves...
