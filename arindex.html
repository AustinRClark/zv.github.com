Contribute

Found a bug? Have a good idea for improving Artifact? Furtive souls may wander into the foetid depths of the github. To reach into eldritch tomes beyond accursed mortal comprehension you can email the developer at zephyr.pellerin (*at*) g mail dot com. Head over to Flask's github page and create a new ticket or fork. If you just want to chat with fellow developers, visit the IRC channel or join the mailinglist. Also as a simple method to help us improve the framework, visit the feedback website. You can also directly add issues and feature requests to the issue tracker.
